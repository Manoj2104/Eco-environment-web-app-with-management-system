About Re-Earth

Building a sustainable tomorrow through community-driven clean-up missions.

Re-Earth is a social platform for environmental volunteers to share, discover, and participate in local eco-events like clean-up drives, tree planting, and awareness campaigns.

Whether you're a solo volunteer or part of a group, Re-Earth empowers you to track contributions, earn badges, and inspire others to protect our planet.
